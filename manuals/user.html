Welcome to the Minimalist Network!

We have assembled a few instructions and videos to help you along the way. Let's get started!

How to sign up

Go to the signup page and enter your details. Make sure to enter all required fields (marked with a red asterisk). You can edit your profile after signing up.

Log in

Go to the login page. Enter your email address and password. You will be redirected to your profile after log in.

Explore

Get inspired by other members! You can explore the profiles and possessions of other members. Clicking the "Explore" link in the navigation menu will take you to the member list.

Upload

First, log in to your account. Then go to the upload page. Images of your possessions can be uploaded either by clicking the upload box or by dragging images onto the box. You can upload multiple images at once. The upload will start immediately. Once finished, visit your profile to see the uploaded images.
The newest things will show first on the member list.

Edit things

First, log into your account. Next, go to your profile page. Click the edit icon in the upper right corner of a thing to edit it. You can change the title and description. You can also specify if the thing is a container with the checkbox. Just click the save button to finalise your edit.

Re-order things

First, log into your account and go to your profile page. The things on your profile can be re-ordered via drag and drop. The order will be saved automatically.

Search

Searching other members is easy! Just type in the search bar at the top of the member list.

Log out

Logging out is simple. Just click the logout link in the navigation menu at the top right.
